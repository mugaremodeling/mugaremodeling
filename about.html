Our Story

MuGa Remodeling

MuGa Remodeling was built on a simple but powerful commitment: deliver the quality of work you'd want in your own home. Founded by Alejandro Reyes and Bryant Pino, the company has grown into one of Tampa Bay's most trusted names in residential remodeling and renovation.

As lead contractors and owners, Alejandro and Bryant bring hands-on expertise to every project — personally overseeing quality control and maintaining direct communication with every client. Their philosophy is straightforward: do the job right, be honest about timelines and pricing, and never cut corners.

"Every project we take on carries our names and our reputation. That means we don't leave until the work is done to a standard we're proud to stand behind — every single time." — Alejandro Reyes & Bryant Pino, Owners
